## What We Track

This website uses **Umami** analytics to understand aggregate traffic trends such as:

- Total visitors
- Page views
- Referrers
- Device and browser categories

We do not use analytics for advertising profiles or cross-site behavioral tracking.

No directly identifying personal data is intentionally collected through Umami event tracking on this site.

## Why We Track

Analytics data is used only to improve site content, structure, and accessibility quality.

The footer visitor number is fetched from Umami aggregated website statistics and shown as a simple count.

For platform-level data handling details, please refer to Umami's own privacy and terms documentation.
