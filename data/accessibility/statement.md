## Commitment

This website is designed and maintained to support inclusive access for people with different abilities and assistive technology needs.

## Conformance Status

This site aims to align with [WCAG 2.2 Level AA](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/). Some content or interactions may still be under improvement, and ongoing updates are made as issues are identified.

## Measures Taken

- Semantic HTML structure for major sections.
- Keyboard-focusable controls and visible focus states.
- Readable type scale and contrast-aware text tones.
- Ongoing automated accessibility checks in development.
